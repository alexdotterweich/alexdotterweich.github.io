How to Use YouTube Converter Plus

Searching for Music

  1. Enter the name of an artist that you like in the search bar
  2. Click the search tab in the navigation bar
  3. Browse suggested songs based on your artist preference

Selecting Music

  1. Click on the search tab in the navigation bar
  2. Scroll through song suggestions
  3. Select ones you like by clicking on the album artwork
  4. Once clicked, these songs will immediately be downloaded

Not sure which artist to search?

  1. Navigate to the Top Artist page
  2. Scroll through these artists until you see one you like
  3. Click on the picture of that artist
  4. Prepare to be redirected to the search page for that artist

How are these songs downloaded?

They are converted to MP3's from YouTube videos and then downloaded to your computer. The process is both legal and safe.
